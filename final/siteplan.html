Site Plan: Ibesikpo Stake Gathering Place

Site Name

Ibesikpo Stake Gathering Place — This name directly represents the purpose and community served by the site: the gathering place of the Ibesikpo Stake. It is clear, location-specific, and spiritually significant. (Optional domain: ibesikpogathering.org)

Site Purpose

The website will serve as a digital hub for the Ibesikpo Stake Gathering Place. It will provide information about the gathering place, leadership, ongoing courses, and a gallery of past events. Additionally, it will feature a registration form for new or interested individuals to join the gathering activities.

Scenarios

  • How can I register to participate in Ibesikpo Stake Gathering Place activities?
  • Who are the leaders of the gathering place and what courses are currently available?

Color Schema

  • Primary – #ffffff: Used as the background color for the site.
  • Secondary – #000000: Used for standard text across all pages.
  • Accent 1 – #66a0b5: Used for section highlights, cards, and hover effects.
  • Accent 2 – #007da5: Used for headings, buttons, borders, and emphasis areas.

Typography

  • Nunito: Used for all headings, body text, and interface elements. It provides a soft, welcoming, and clean visual aesthetic that aligns with the community spirit of the site.
